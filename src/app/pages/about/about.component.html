In a time where the world looks at Africa for inspiration, it is interesting to observe what, where and how we as Africans do. Often having to re-appropriate our own culture against the commercial viability the West has over us, we seldom look past aestheticism to unwrap the true nature of we are.

Informed by research that encompasses critical and speculative theory, cultural fusion, spiritual literature and historical sources, WANDA LEPHOTO explores a distinctive notion of luxury dress, looking to merge global nuances with distinctively African cultures, traditions and approaches.

We are here to tell a story.
